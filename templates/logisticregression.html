Stroke Prediction

Stroke prediction assesses an individual's risk of having a stroke. It helps healthcare professionals identify high-risk individuals and implement preventive measures.

The following variables as input or predictor variables: age, heart disease, work type, average glucose level and body mass index (bmi). These variables were chosen based to predict the probability of having stroke

HOW TO USE

To use this stroke prediction application, simply input the relevant information and click "predict" to obtain an estimation of the probability of having stroke.
